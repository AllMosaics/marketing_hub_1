Our Sourcing

Directly from Murano

Smalti is the oldest continuous glass art tradition in the world. The island of Murano has been the centre of European glassmaking since the 13th century, when the Venetian Republic ordered all furnaces moved there to protect the city from fire.

We source directly from two family-owned furnaces who have made smalti using the same mineral pigment and wood-ash flux recipes for generations. The result is a material of extraordinary depth and luminosity — unlike any industrially-produced tile.
