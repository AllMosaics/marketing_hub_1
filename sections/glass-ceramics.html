The Materials

Glass & ceramic,
precisely made

Vitreous glass tiles are made by casting molten glass with mineral colourants into sheets, then cutting to a consistent 2×2cm size. The flat back bonds reliably to any substrate; the slightly textured face holds grout beautifully and scatters light in a way that painted or printed tiles simply cannot.

Our hand-fired ceramic tesserae are made by independent Australian and European studios. Each piece is individually thrown, fired, and cut — no two are exactly alike, giving finished work a warmth and presence impossible to achieve with industrial tile.
